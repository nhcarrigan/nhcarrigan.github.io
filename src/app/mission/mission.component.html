Our Mission

Here at NHCarrigan, we believe that people should have access to the tools and resources to reach their goals.

As such, the work we do follows these core values:

  • We strive to build affordable products for start-ups to help them manage their product and community.
  • We help build safe and welcoming communities for folks looking to create their own space.
  • We create opportunities for developers to break in to the tech industry.

These values are reflected in the clients we take on and the projects we will accept.
